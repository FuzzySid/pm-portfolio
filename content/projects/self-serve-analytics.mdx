## The Problem

The data team was spending 60% of their time on reactive, ad hoc data pulls for the operations team. Most requests were variations of the same 5–6 questions asked repeatedly at different time ranges or breakdowns.

## Approach

I ran a structured audit of 3 months of Jira tickets and Slack requests, categorizing each by question type, requester, and frequency. This revealed that 80% of volume came from 6 recurring question patterns — all answerable with a well-designed self-serve dashboard.

## Design Process

1. Co-designed with ops leads using low-fidelity Figma prototypes
2. Validated metric definitions with the data team before building
3. Soft launched to 3 power users for 2 weeks before broad rollout
4. Trained 22 operations staff in 3 office hours sessions

## Results

- Ad hoc data requests: 47/week → 9/week
- Dashboard weekly active users: 34 of 38 eligible ops staff (89% adoption)
- Time-to-insight for common questions: 2 days → under 5 minutes
